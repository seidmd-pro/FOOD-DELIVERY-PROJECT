
## Actors

### Primary Actors (Human Users)
1. **Customer** - Registered users who can browse restaurants, place orders, make payments, and track deliveries
2. **Guest Customer** - Unregistered users with limited browsing capabilities (view restaurants and menus)
3. **Restaurant Manager** - Users who manage restaurant profiles, menus, orders, and business analytics
4. **Delivery Driver** - Users responsible for picking up and delivering orders to customers
5. **System Administrator** - Users with administrative privileges to manage the entire platform

### Secondary Actors (External Systems)
6. **Payment Gateway (telebirr)** - External service for processing secure payments
7. **Uber Eats API** - External service providing additional delivery options and expanded coverage
8. **Google Maps API** - External service for location services, geocoding, and route optimization
