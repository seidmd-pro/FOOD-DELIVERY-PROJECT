
         ## Actors
         
 Actor is anything that interacts with the system.
Actors can be:
  - People (users of the app)
  - Other systems or APIs (external services your system depends on)

### Primary Actors (Human Users)
1. Customer :
- Registered users who can browse restaurants, place orders, make payments, and track deliveries
 Who they are: Registered users with an account.
 What they can do:
    - Browse restaurants and menus
    - Add food to cart
    - Place orders
    - Make payments
    - Track order status and delivery
    - View order history

2. Guest Customer :
- Unregistered users with limited browsing capabilities (view restaurants and menus)
Who they are: Users without an account.

What they can do:
   - View restaurants
   - View menus
   - Check prices and availability
Limitations:
   - Cannot place orders
   - Cannot make payments
   - Cannot track deliveries

3. Restaurant Manager :
- Users who manage restaurant profiles, menus, orders, and business analytics

Who they are: Owners or staff managing a restaurant on the platform.

What they can do:
     - Create and update restaurant profiles
     - Add, edit, or remove menu items
     - Accept or reject orders
     - Update order status (preparing, ready, etc.)
     - View sales reports and analytics

4. Delivery Driver :
- Users responsible for picking up and delivering orders to customers
Who they are: People responsible for delivering food.

What they can do:
    - Accept delivery requests
    - View pickup and drop-off locations
    - Update delivery status (picked up, on the way, delivered)
    - Use navigation tools

5. System Administrator :
 - Users with administrative privileges to manage the entire platform
Who they are: Platform administrators (not normal users).

What they can do:
       - Manage user accounts (customers, drivers, restaurants)
       - Handle complaints and disputes
       - Monitor system performance
       - Control platform rules and permissions
       - Ensure security and reliability

### Secondary Actors (External Systems)

6. Payment Gateway (telebirr) :
- External service for processing secure payments

What it does:
      - Processes payments securely
      - Handles transactions and confirmations
      - Prevents fraud
